Pancakes

Description

Look no further because a steaming stack of perfectly soft, best fluffy pancakes are right here! weekends will never be the same again.

Ingriedents

  • 2 cups all purpose | plain flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar or sweetener
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg

Steps

  1. Combine together
  2. Use a wire whisk to whisk the wet ingredients
  3. Set the batter aside and allow to rest
  4. Heat a nonstick pan or griddle
  5. When the underside is golden and bubbles begin to appear on the surface, flip with a spatula and cook until golden
